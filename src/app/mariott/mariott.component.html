Paris Mariott Ambasador Hotel

Create lasting memories at our Paris Opera District hotel

Paris Marriott Opera Ambassador Hotel welcomes you to the City of Light with contemporary style, superb service and a wonderful location. Our hotel is situated on the Boulevard Haussmann, in the heart of the Opera District; attractions including the Opera Garnier and Galeries Lafayette are moments away. Meticulously designed, Art Deco-inspired accommodation offers stylish furnishings, complimentary Wi-Fi and sleek, well-lit bathrooms. Additional amenities include a modern gym and an Executive Lounge for guests reserving upgraded rooms and suites. Visit 16 Haussmann, our signature restaurant, for artfully prepared French dishes in a welcoming atmosphere. With several versatile venues, we are a premier destination for business meetings and intimate social events. You'll be thrilled with our close proximity to many of the city's most exciting things to see and do. We look forward to your stay at Paris Marriott Opera Ambassador Hotel.
