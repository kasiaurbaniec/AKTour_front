Hotel Zacisze Wąchock

Zacisze Hotel is a new, comfortable building located in the city center of Wąchock. A unique place for individual and business guests, as well as families with children. Surroundings of the hotel allow you to find a moment of peace, relax, get away from the noise and breathe in the fresh mountain air. We guarantee a pleasant atmosphere, quality service, comfort and delicious cuisine. The hotel contains 10 accommodation units: 2 standard rooms, 7 studio rooms and a suit. There is also a restaurant and conference & banquet hall.
